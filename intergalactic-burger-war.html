Overview

Build burgers, wage war! Intergalactic Burger War is a fast-n-wacky card game where players compete to build the best damn burger in the Cosmos. Gather the galaxy's greatest ingredients while sabotaging your fellow extraterrestrial playersâ€“all is fair in burgers and war.

Intergalactic Burger War is the first game I ever made. It taught me the essence of game design: crafting an experience greater than the sum of its parts. I gained firsthand experience with the iterative design process, continuously playtesting and revising my creation. It taught me aspects of graphic design and UI/UX: packing dense information into an appealing medium to be read, learned, and experienced.

Genre: Card Game (3-8 players)

Team Size: Sole Creator

Tools Used: Illustrator

Status: Finished
