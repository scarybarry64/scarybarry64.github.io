Last Lunar Air

2021 - 2024

A Gameboy-inspired side-scroller about skateboarding on the Moon, developed entirely solo. Oxygen's low, so do tricks and get air to get air.

Built in Unity with a custom physics engine, retro spritework, and original SFX/music. Made for my senior capstone, further expanded on since then.
