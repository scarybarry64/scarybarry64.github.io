Sally Says Die!
Overview

Rodney Rodent gets to work at Rodney's Pizzeria, expecting a run of the mill kids' birthday party. He gets more than he bargained for when he pleases the spoiled birthday girl, who unleashes a crowd of unusual children to catch him and keep him for herself! Can he escape the mall before it's too late?

This game was made in 48 hours by a team of 3 for the 2024 Global Game Jam. The team consisted of myself (programmer), Andrew Vien (level and sound designer), and Rylie Parker (art). The game and all of it's art assets were made from scratch in those 48 hours. Bug-fixes and extras were added after.

Genre: 2D Topdown Puzzle-Platformer

Team Size: 3

Tools Used: Unity, C#

Status: Released
